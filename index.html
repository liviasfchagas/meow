Meow

a meow is a high-pitched sound that cats make to communicate with humans - cats may meow for many reasons, including to say hello, ask for something, or to indicate that something is wrong

why cats meow:

  • greeting: cats may meow to say "hi" or "where have you been?"
  • attention: cats may meow to get your attention for play, petting, or treats
  • food: cats may meow if their food bowl is empty
  • illness: cats may meow at night if they have an illness like kidney disease, urinary tract problems, or arthritis
  • stress: cats may meow when they are stressed
  • environmental changes: cats may meow if there are changes in their environment, such as a new house, pet, or baby
